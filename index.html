About us

UI RUG is a group of R enthusiasts based in the University of Ibadan, Nigeria. We have held several meetups since the year 2011 at the University of Ibadan. We have been instrumental in training several people including staff, students, researchers, data scientists and industry practitioners on the use of R. We are the pioneer R trainers for all Nigeria's University faculties at the National Mathematical Centre Abuja (NMC) since 2015. Our lead facilitator Olalekan Obisesan was a trainer on 'Environmental Statistics with R' at the AIMS South Africa Muizenberg in 2017. We initially had a group called the 'R moving train' on facebook and now on Twitter as UI R Users Group.

Who we are

UIRUG is a dynamic R User group focused on spreading the news of R and the latest development in the technology space to its community.We connect regularly for trainings,webinars and pride ourselves in linking up community members with opportunities that will help them.Our events are free and open to all

What is R

R is a programming language for statistical computing, data analysis and visualization of data. R is free,open source and has over two million users worldwide.Lots of industries have adopted the R language as a data science platform,including biotech, finance and research industries.click here to more about R.
